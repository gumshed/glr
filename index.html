About Us

501(c)(3) Nonprofit Sphere
of The Movement DAO

Founded in 2017, The Movement DAO has been a grassroots and globally collaborative project focused on creating value for humanity's benefit.

As a Nonprofit, Greenpoint Land Reserves is creating a land reserve system that protects and reclaims the natural wonder while supporting the people living in surrounding communities. We consider access to Public Lands a Public Good.

Movement DAO members can participate in Board meetings and control the direction democratically by voting.

How It Works

DAO Controlled Land Holdings

Land AcquirementLand Management Team

Greenpoint Land Reserves (GLR) identifies lands with varying degrees of intrinsic value and weighs them against the surrounding communities' current socio-economic needs.

Preservation +Greenpoint Land Trust

Once acquired, property is placed into a land trust which states the DAO as Benefactor. GLR is named a Trustee and acts as Asset Manager for the DAO.

Public GoodsCrypto Land Preserve

Built as a bridge, this leverage-able land trust rewards members while creating a physical store of value held by the DAO.
